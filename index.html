Set your email font to Arial, 11pt, black. For most clients you just paste, set the font to Arial, and save. Setup steps are right below.
Setup — 3 steps (Outlook, Apple Mail)

Step 1 — Copy your signature

  1. Find your card below and click Copy signature.

Step 2 — Paste it into your mail app

Outlook / Office 365:

  1. Go to Settings → Mail → Compose and reply → Email signature.
  2. Click into the signature box and paste.
  3. Assign it to new messages and replies/forwards, then Save.

iPhone / Apple Mail:

  1. Open Mail → Settings → Signatures and add a new signature.
  2. Uncheck "Always match my default message font."
  3. Paste. The logo comes with it automatically.

Step 3 — Set your email font to Arial, 11pt, black

This sets the font your emails are written in, so every message matches the signature.

Outlook on the web / new Outlook:

  1. Go to Settings → Mail → Compose and reply.
  2. Scroll to Message format.
  3. Set the font to Arial, size 11, color black, then Save.

Outlook desktop (classic):

  1. Go to File → Options → Mail → Stationery and Fonts.
  2. Under New mail messages, click Font and choose Arial, Regular, 11, Automatic (black).
  3. Repeat under Replying or forwarding messages, then click OK on each window.

Mail on Mac:

  1. Go to Mail → Settings → Fonts & Colors.
  2. Next to Message font, click Select and choose Arial, 11.

iPhone: nothing to set — the default font is fine.
